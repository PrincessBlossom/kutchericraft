    Help & FAQ

    KatcheriCraft is a tool that helps Carnatic musicians build and evaluate concert setlists according to the Ariyakkudi Ramanuja Iyengar format. It checks for structure, variety, and classical formatting, and it will give you feedback and a score.

    Use the form above to enter details about each song (title, raga, tala, etc.). Click "Add Song" to include it in the setlist. Feedback and scoring will appear automatically.

    • Varnam (if used) must be first
    • Thillana (if used) must be in the last 3 items
    • No raga reuse
    • Warnings for excessive use of the same tala, tempo, or composer
    • Feedback-based score out of 100

    Future features may include raga similarity checks, save/load functionality, theme filters, and export options.
